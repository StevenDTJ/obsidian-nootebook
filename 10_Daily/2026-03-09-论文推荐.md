### 1. [Cost-effectiveness of a single dose of the adjuvanted RSVPreF3 vaccine for the prevention of RSV among patients with COPD in Italy](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41631651/)
- **作者**: Anna Puggina, Filippo Rumi, Eleftherios Zarkadoulas, et al.
- **期刊**: Expert Review of Vaccines
- **发表日期**: 2026-12
- **数据源**: PubMed
- **匹配领域**: [[卫生政策]], [[卫生经济学]]
- **匹配关键词**: cost-effectiveness, health system, QALY, public health, hospital
- **综合评分**: 2.40/3.00
- **摘要**: 评估了在意大利60-74岁COPD患者中接种单剂RSVPreF3疫苗的公共卫生影响和成本效果。使用静态多队列Markov模型，发现疫苗可减少29%的RSV相关急性呼吸道感染和38%的住院/死亡，ICER为€1,306/QALY，具有成本效果。
- **详细笔记**: [[RSV vaccine cost-effectiveness in COPD patients Italy]]

### 2. [Geographic disparities and health inequality evolution in the disease burden of TBL cancer in Asia](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41630487/)
- **作者**: Hongming Wang, Xuebo Qin, Xing'e Zhang, et al.
- **期刊**: Annals of Medicine
- **发表日期**: 2026-12
- **数据源**: PubMed
- **匹配领域**: [[公共经济学]], [[卫生政策]], [[卫生经济学]], [[健康公平]], [[健康不平等]]
- **匹配关键词**: inequality, DALY, health inequality, health equity, concentration index, healthcare access
- **综合评分**: 2.40/3.00
- **摘要**: 基于GBD 2023数据，分析亚洲TBL癌症负担的地理差异和时间趋势。发现健康不平等加剧（SII从275.8升至311.6，CI持续为负），负担集中在低HDI地区。预测到2038年ASPR将增加28.7%。
- **详细笔记**: [[Geographic disparities and health inequality in TBL cancer in Asia]]

### 3. [Modeling the potential public health and economic impact of COVID-19 vaccination strategies in Costa Rica](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41711397/)
- **作者**: Juan José Baldi-Castro, Carlos Fernando Mendoza, Moe H Kyaw, et al.
- **期刊**: Expert Review of Vaccines
- **发表日期**: 2026-12
- **数据源**: PubMed
- **匹配领域**: [[卫生政策]], [[卫生经济学]]
- **匹配关键词**: QALY, cost-effectiveness, public health, hospital
- **综合评分**: 2.35/3.00
- **摘要**: 使用Markov决策树模型评估哥斯达黎加不同COVID-19疫苗接种策略的公共卫生和经济影响。针对≥65岁老年人和18-64岁高风险人群的接种策略预计可节省总直接和社会成本超过1000万美元。
- **详细笔记**: [[COVID-19 vaccination cost-effectiveness in Costa Rica]]

### 4. [Mental health burdens, typhoon and structural health inequities: evidence from a global perspective](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41550028/)

- **期刊**: Global Health Action
- **发表日期**: 2026-12
- **数据源**: PubMed

- **匹配关键词**: social determinant, inequality, DALY, public health, health expenditure
- **综合评分**: 2.27/3.00
- **摘要**: 基于健康社会决定因素理论，评估台风暴露对心理健康障碍负担的影响。发现高收入、教育、HDI和公共卫生投资与更强的心理韧性相关，而低收入、高自付费用和高不平等则表明脆弱性。
